About

As a child, I would spend hours gazing at the sky, thrilled by the faint lights of satellites drifting across the night. That feeling of wonder and discovery has always stayed with me. I created STARGAZER to share that excitement with everyone, not just scientists.

This platform aims to make the joy of satellite watching accessible to everyone, helping you know exactly when and where to spot these fascinating celestial objects. Whether you're a seasoned enthusiast or someone new to the skies, STARGAZER brings the stars closer to you.

Perfect for kids and families, STARGAZER offers a fun and educational way to connect with the wonders of the universe. It's a chance to bond over stargazing, spark curiosity, and create lasting memories under the stars. Let's reignite the thrill of stargazing and make it a part of your everyday life.

So Look Up. Be Curious.
